Our Story

Foi en Lui — French for "Faith in Him" — was born from a simple belief: your clothing can be a daily reminder of God's promises.

Every piece in our collection carries a Bible verse — not just as decoration, but as a declaration. When you put on a Foi en Lui hoodie, you're wrapping yourself in truth.

We believe faith should be worn boldly. Not shouted, but shown. In the quality of what we make, in the verses we carry, and in the way we treat every person who wears our name.
